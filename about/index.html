About

The Blog

This is a website/blog used to post all manner of musings and thoughts about the crypto currency space. The slant for most of the postings will be of a technical nature, however, philosophical rants will also be prominent

Myself

I am a software developer from South Africa who first got exposed to the internet in the early 90s - I actual made a brochure website for the original httpd and netscape combination. I have written CGI scripts in perl and C, ASPX in VB and been through the ringer and back. I love technology. I love IT and I love solving software puzzles. It is this same love that has led me to look into the Cryptocurrency area - to be honest the price volatility really doesn't bother me as I am more interested in the issues related to utility and scalability; if these are taken care of the price will take care of itself.

Come with me and enjoy the ride.
